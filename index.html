About Me

top

I began my journey with computers when my father sat me in front of his old Commodore 64 computer and showed me how to start a game. Around that time, I was exposed to Apple IIe computers and learned how to control a computer a little bit more, writing simple programs to complete math lessons. When I started middle-school, I really started to dive into programming and writing little snippets to solve problems I needed solving.

I am currently a student with Thinkful and participate in several coding challenges when I find them. In my free time, I play videos games, which a good number of them can be modified to do things the developers did not originally intend. From Doom to Minecraft, I have been exposed to several different languages or derivatives that allowed for some interesting, fun and informative moments.

If you seek a developer to complete a job or join your team, look no further! Let me know what it is that I can do for you.
